About Skillraace

Our Story

Skillraace is dedicated to empowering students through practical, industry-relevant education. Established in February 2024, our journey began with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry demands. SKILLRAACE is a leading provider of online education, dedicated to empowering individuals through accessible and interactive learning experiences. We believe in the transformative power of education and strive to make quality learning resources available to anyone, anywhere.

Why Choose Us?

Skillraace stands as a beacon of trust and quality. We are a proud **MSME-registered**. we are AICTE-approved and committed to shaping students into industry-ready professionals through hands-on learning and expert guidance. We offer carefully designed courses, internships, and training programs that bridge the gap between academics and real-world industry skills. Our expert mentors provide personalized support, ensuring every student grows with confidence. With a strong focus on practical knowledge, placement guidance, and soft skills, we prepare you to excel in your career. Choosing Skillraace means choosing a trusted, AICTE-approved partner in your journey toward success.
